Questions About Layaway
How does your layaway program work?

Our layaway program is simple! Just pay 10% of the item’s price as a non-refundable down payment. You then have 30 days to make another payment of 30% of the item’s price or you will lose the 10% deposit. Each subsequent payment towards your items becomes part of the non-refundable deposit until you have completely paid for your item. (Note: on the final payment of 30% towards the item’s price, sales tax will also have to be collected, making your final payment greater than the previous two.)

What if I don’t pay my item off completely? Do I lose all my money?

The deposit is non-refundable. However, we will allow you to transfer whatever money you have paid down on an item, less the cancellation fee of $10, to store credit to be used towards the purchase of any other item in our store. We will not be able to refund you your deposit under any circumstance, but you can pick out a different item if you change your mind about the one on layaway. (Note: Store credit expires one year from the day it was issued.)

What if I can’t pay on time but still intend to buy the item?

If you are still intending to buy the item but will be a few days late (no more than 5) on your next payment, just call us and let us know. We will be happy to extend the due date of your next payment as a one-time courtesy.

Are there some items you do not offer layaway on?

Yes. We only offer layaway on items priced at $50 or more. Also, we reserve the right to not offer layaway on certain items, although this is pretty rare. Some examples of items we could choose to not offer layaway on include hot-selling items, items which depreciate rapidly (such as some electronics), and some large-ticket items we sell (such as cars, boats, and 4-wheelers).

Can I make layaway payments by phone?

Yes. There is no need to come by our store every month if you don’t want to. Feel free to call us with a debit/credit card number, and we will be happy to take your payment over the phone.

Can I have multiple items on layaway?

Yes. There is no limit to the number of layaway accounts you can have open with us. Feel free to layaway any item you have interest in.

Is the layaway program free? Are there any hidden costs or fees?

The layaway program is completely free as long as you follow through and purchase the item. If you do not purchase the item and your deposit money is transferred to in-store credit, there is a cancellation fee of $10. This covers the cost of us storing your item for several months in our warehouse.

Any layaway requires a 10% non-refundable deposit. If you come back within 90 days and want to cancel your layaway, we will refund you anything in excess of the 10% in cash or store credit. However, if the 90-day period elapses and the layaway expires, you will lose any money paid toward the layaway. Please contact us if you need an extended grace period on your layaway and we will be happy to work with you, within reason.
