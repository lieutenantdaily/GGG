Questions About Other Loans
What is a payday loan?

A payday loan is a short-term, small dollar amount loan you intend to pay back once you receive your next paycheck. We know there are many situations that come up in between paychecks which you may not have been expecting. At Gold, Guns and Guitars we can help you solve those short-term financial situations by loaning you up to $500 on a 10-30 day term.

What do I need to get a loan?

You will need to fill out a short loan application and verify your income (pay stub, W-2, etc.). Our staff will be able to help walk you through this process and if you are approved we can usually have you out the door, money in hand, in under half an hour.

Can I renew my loan if I can’t pay it off?

Yes! You will need to notify us two business days before the due date and we will be happy to roll the loan over. Just pay any amount between the minimum of the accrued interest and the full amount of the loan, and we will be happy to extend the remainder of your loan through another loan cycle.

Will my information be kept private?

Absolutely! Between confidentiality laws, our cutting edge software, and internal security measures, you can rest assured your information will be kept secret and safe with us.

Whether it’s our friendly and knowledgeable staff, our lenient and understanding loan renewal policies, or the fact we have some of the lowest interest rates in town, we are confident that you will find Gold, Guns and Guitars to be the best option for you!
