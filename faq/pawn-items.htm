What items can I pawn or sell?
We loan money on, or outright buy, most items of value. Here are some examples:
  • Jewelry: gold and silver jewelry, coins, bullion, dental gold, gold metals, scrap gold, etc.

  • Weapons: rifles, pistols, shotguns, bows, crossbows, paintball guns, knives, swords, etc. All weapons must be in working order, and if it’s a gun, please provide a magazine.

  • Tools: hand tools, air tools, power tools, compressors, ladders, drywall tools, carpet tools, tool boxes, welders, pressure washers, mowers, chainsaws, weed eaters, tillers, generators, space heaters, air conditioners, etc. Tools must be in working order and contain battery and charger when applicable.

  • Audio/Visual: HDTVs (must have remote and must not be older than 5 years), blu-ray players, DVD players, digital cameras and camcorders (must have charger), home and portable stereos, iPods and MP3 players, etc.

  • Musical Instruments: guitars, banjos, trumpets, violins, flutes, drums, clarinets, saxophones, keyboards, amps, microphones, etc. We prefer that instruments have cases, but we will make you an offer on an instrument without a case as well.

  • Video Games: We accept most game consoles and games (even older models).

  • Computers: desktops and laptops (newer models only). Must have an LCD monitor (any size accepted).

  • Electronics: eReaders, iPads, tablets, and most cell phones are accepted (must be free and clear from a contract).

  • Vehicles: four wheelers (ATVs), dirt bikes, go-karts, small boats, and cars of all makes and models. A car with a clean title is preferred for pawns. We either allow that you leave the car with us, or complete a title loan.

  • College Textbooks: We also offer pawns on college textbooks that are still in edition! We will offer a price based on the condition of the book, and let you use your books to get quick cash! Please ask us about college textbook buybacks!

  • Other: binoculars, fishing tackle, major brand golf clubs, DVD movies, advanced calculators, mini refrigerators, vacuum cleaners, and MANY MORE ITEMS… just ask if you don’t see your item on this list!
