About Us

Gold, Guns and Guitars has taken pawn shops to another level! Step inside our store and see the difference: we are everything any other pawn shop around isn’t… and that’s a good thing. We are new, modern, clean, and organized.

More than anything, we are affordable. You are at a pawn shop to look for a deal, and that’s what we are here to provide for you.

We can also buy items you want to pawn, as well as loan on it. Bring in your jewelry, guns, electronic items, tools or anything else of value and we will make you the best cash offer possible.

Besides pawning, we also offer high-quality gunsmithing. Our services include gun cleaning, scope mounting, bore sighting and much more! All this for $40 per hour, or a low flat fee, depending on the service. 

Our Guarantee

From the moment you step into Gold, Guns and Guitars, you are our top priority! We want to make sure you have a quality shopping experience. Our employees are here for you; they are friendly and well-trained. Our goal is to keep you coming back!

We are committed to our "No Jerk Behind the Counter Guarantee!"

Our store is transparent. We post everything about how much we charge for pawn, title pawn, check cashing, payday advance and much more on our website. We will also give quotes directly over the phone for almost any item! 

We guarantee no limit on ammo purchases…Ever! If we have it in stock, you can buy it. All of it. In fact, we’ll even let you pre-order ammo and call you when it comes in. We carry every caliber and type of ammo possible and order it by the truckload.
